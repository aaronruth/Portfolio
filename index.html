Project "GL08TY"

Project "GL08TY" is a one-of-one Mercedes 380sl. Originally powered by a euro-spec 3.8L V8, the two door coupe is getting a full makeover. The car will be powered by a J-spec Toyota 2JZ-GTE that has been converted from twin turbo to single 66mm turbo. This build project features not only a full wiring tuck and engine bay shave, but a completely custom wiring harness from front bumper to rear, a custom sport inspired interior, and several subtle hints towards craftsmanship and performance.
